# New York Life Insurance (new-york-life-insurance)

New York Life Insurance Company is the largest mutual life insurance company in the United States, founded in 1845. The company provides whole, term, universal, and variable universal life insurance, long-term care and disability insurance, annuities, and asset management through New York Life Investments.
